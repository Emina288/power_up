Wind power plant

Wind energy is the fastest growing mode of electricity production across the planet.It is the most widely used source of renewable enegry. Wind energy is a clean source of power that causes minimal pollution and mass production!

How do they work?

  1. Wind blows past a wind turbine
  2. The blades of a wind turbine captures the wind's kinetic energy and rotate, turning the kinetic energy into mechanical energy!
  3. The rotation turns an internal shaft connected to a gearbox, which increases the speed of rotation by a factor of 100!
  4. That spins a rotor in a generator that produces electricity

Solar power plant

Solar power has so much potential that there is enough solar energy hitting the Earth every hour to meet all of humanity's power needs for the entire year. It is a 100% clean, renewable energy source. The best part is that the cost of solar panels has dropped in recent years, more than 70% in the last decade so installing solar panels in any household brings so many benefits, for the client as well as the environment!
