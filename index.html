Welcome!

This a demo is created to visualize data of electricity production by four different sources.

  • By clicking on one of the four windows, we can see an animated bar chart that is showing a percentage of how much out of total energy production is being produced by the source we selected
  • By hovering over one of the bars, we can read the production of other countries regarding the hovered bar
  • By clicking on one of the bars, we can see an animated line bar that is visualizing the production of the energy over the years for the country we clicked on
  • We can also click more the get some more information on the selected type of a power plant
